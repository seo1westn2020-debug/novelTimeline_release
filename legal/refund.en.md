## 1. Trial period (not subject to refund)

- novel-timeline offers a **90-day (3-month) free trial** starting from the first installation.
- No payment is collected during the trial, so the trial itself is not subject to refund.
- If the User does not convert to a paid subscription after the trial ends, the app enters Read-only mode (data remains intact, exports remain available) and no automatic charge occurs.

## 2. Paid subscription refund window

| SKU | Refund window | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Monthly Subscription | **Within 14 days** of payment | After the refund window, cancel the subscription before the next billing date (access continues until the end of the paid period). |
| Annual Subscription | **Within 14 days** of payment | Same principle. Post-1-month pro-rata rule: see Section 4. |

Both subscriptions share the **same 14-day refund window** for clarity and to meet EU/EEA/UK consumer-protection standards.

## 3. Refund eligibility

You may request a refund if one or more of the following apply:

- You did not receive a working license key.
- The product materially differs from its advertised features or documentation.
- A serious defect prevents normal use, and the Provider cannot resolve it within a reasonable time.
- Change of mind (within the refund window in Section 2).

## 4. Refund limitations

Refunds may be denied, or only partially granted, in the following cases:

- Request submitted after the refund window expires.
- Evidence that the User has publicly shared or redistributed the license key.
- The User has violated the Terms of Service (reverse engineering, illegal content generation, etc.).
- For Annual Subscriptions used for **more than one month**, only the unused portion (after deducting the used months) may be refunded.

## 5. How to request a refund

Email `askNovelTimeline@gmail.com` with the following information:

1. **Name on the order** (as used in Lemon Squeezy checkout)
2. **Order ID** — found in the Lemon Squeezy receipt email
3. **Reason for the refund**
4. **Last 4 digits of the payment card** (for verification; optional)

You will receive a status response within **3 business days** of submission.

## 6. Processing time

- Approved refunds are issued through Lemon Squeezy to the original payment method.
- Depending on your card issuer or bank, the refund may take **5–10 business days** to appear on your statement.

## 7. Subscription cancellation (separate from refund)

- When you cancel a subscription, you retain full access **until the end of the already-paid period**, after which the app enters Read-only mode. No separate refund is issued in this case.
- You can cancel any time via the **"Manage subscription"** link in your Lemon Squeezy receipt email or through the customer portal — no email request required.

## 8. Relationship to statutory rights

- This policy does not limit the **statutory withdrawal rights** guaranteed to consumers under Korea's *Act on Consumer Protection in Electronic Commerce*.
- For consumers in the EU / EEA / UK, additional rights under local consumer-protection laws may apply.
